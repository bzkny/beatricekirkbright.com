The Design Processes

Problem

My first task at Aon was to make a client facing web simulation mobile-ready. There was an industry conference coming up and our business wanted to position itself competitively. Here was what it looked like:

No branding

Poor UX on input fields

No consistency
— internal to the page
or
— external across other pages

The Other Problems

No design process or tools at all.

The Real Problem

The wicked problem. Systems thinking. Theme-ing was one-off. Design was an after thought. Had to audit and use holistic heuristic for design and development.

Some quick research revealed sooo many problems with the look and feel. Very quickly I started asking myself was mobile readiness really the problem? I had a feeling there was a deeper, 'wicked problem' that had to be identified here.
